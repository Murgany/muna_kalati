Introduction

Welcome to Muna Kalati! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app. We encourage you to read this policy carefully and contact us if you have any questions.

1. Data Collection

We collect limited personal information from children, such as name, age, and username.

2. Parental Consent

We require parental consent before collecting or processing personal information from children.

3. Data Security

We use encryption and secure servers to protect personal information and ensure it is not accessible to unauthorized third parties.

4. Data Usage

We use personal information solely for app improvement, personalized recommendations, and progress tracking.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share personal information with third-party companies or organizations, except for legal or regulatory purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide services or as required by law.

7. Data Protection

We comply with applicable data protection regulations, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

8. Parental Rights

Parents have the right to review, update, or delete their child's personal information upon request.

9. Data Breach

Parents have the right to review, update, or delete their child's personal information upon request.

10. Privacy Policy

We provide a clear and transparent privacy policy explaining our data collection, use, and protection practices.

11. Age-Appropriate

Our app is designed for children aged 4-12, and we ensure our data collection and processing practices are age-appropriate.

12. Compliance

We comply with local and international data protection regulations, including the African Union's data protection guidelines.
